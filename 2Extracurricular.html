Gram has participated in many extracurricular activities, as a Mountain Bike team captain, a news editor, and a German and Arabic language organization general member.

Gram was team captain of his high school mountain bike team and participated in his high school's Youth In Government Mock Trial team.

At Elon, Gram does copy editing and writes for the Pendulum school newspaper and the Elon News Network website.

He is also a member of Elon Club Climbing's Exec board where he serves as secretary, and he participates in Elon's Model UN club, where he is treasurer.
