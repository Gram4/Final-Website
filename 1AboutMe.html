Gram Brownlee is a sophomore at Elon University with an interest and declared major in Journalistic Editing and Political Science. He has worked in public service and has experience writing for mulitiple publications and participating in mock political conferences.

In his free time, Gram likes to mountain bike, hike, and rock climb. He also plays board games, practices music, and writes a lot.

When not at school, Gram lives in Greenville, South Carolina, where he has worked as a student poll manager at his local polling place during the 2020 election cycle. He worked behind the concessions counter at the Pavilion Rec Center in Greenville, SC, where he also worked watching over some of the rec facilities. He also interned with Greenville360, a local entertainment and business blog.

Now that he is going to school at Elon, Gram spends most of his time studying, but also works for Elon's Event Support Staff and Elon's student news oranization, Elon News Network.
